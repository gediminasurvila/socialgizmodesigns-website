A proven process for your branding success

Branding is not overwhelming when you are in the right hands. With my clear, step-by-step process, you’ll always know what’s happening next and you’ll feel supported the entire way.

  • Discovering Your Vision

    Our journey begins with a conversation. Here, I take the time to understand your business, your goals, and the unique qualities of your candle or skincare products. This foundational step ensures that the branding aligns perfectly with your vision and market needs.

  • Crafting Your Story

    Based on our initial discussions, I start the creative process. This involves conceptualising the visual elements that will form your brand identity, from colour schemes and typography to logo and packaging designs. Throughout this stage, your feedback is crucial to refine and perfect the concept.

  • Bringing Ideas to Life

    Once the concept is approved, I dive into the design phase, meticulously crafting each element of your brand identity. This step is all about attention to detail and ensuring that every aspect of the design resonates with your brand’s ethos and appeals to your target audience.

  • Perfecting Your Brand

    Collaboration continues as we review the designs together. Your input is vital here to make any necessary refinements. This iterative process ensures that the final outcome not only meets but exceeds your expectations.

  • Launching Your Brand

    With the designs finalised and approved, I prepare all the necessary files and guidelines for your brand's launch. This includes providing you with all the tools and knowledge you need to maintain brand consistency across all platforms and mediums.

  • Your Ongoing Brand Partner

    Even after your brand is successfully launched, I remain committed to your business's growth. Whether it’s providing additional designs, marketing support, or simply being there to offer advice, consider me your ongoing partner in your brand's journey.
